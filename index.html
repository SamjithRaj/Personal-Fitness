Today's Directives

24-Week Strategic Plan

Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Structural Adaptation

Objective: Re-establish training consistency, improve neuromuscular control, and strengthen knee stability. A moderate caloric deficit will begin the fat loss process without impeding recovery.

Phase 2 (Weeks 5-12): Hypertrophy & Work Capacity

Objective: Maximize muscle growth and increase conditioning. Volume and intensity will increase systematically. Caloric intake will adjust based on progress.

Phase 3 (Weeks 13-20): Strength Intensification

Objective: Increase maximal strength on key lifts while continuing to reduce body fat. Training will focus on heavy compounds and metabolic conditioning.

Phase 4 (Weeks 21-24): Peaking & Hardening

Objective: Achieve peak physical condition. A final, aggressive push to strip remaining body fat and reveal dense, functional muscle.

Phase 1: Nutrition Protocol (No Supplements)

Daily Targets

  • Calories: 2600 kcal
  • Protein: 145g
  • Carbohydrates: 345g
  • Fat: 70g
  • Hydration: 3-4 Liters Minimum

Adjustment Rule

Weigh yourself daily. After 2 weeks, average the weight. If you have not lost 0.5-1 kg, reduce daily calories by 200, primarily from carbohydrates. Recalculate every 2 weeks.

Recovery & Weekly KPIs

Recovery Protocols

  • Sleep: 7-9 hours. Non-negotiable.
  • Mobility: Perform prescribed cool-downs daily. Foam roll major muscle groups on rest days for 10 minutes.
  • Breathing: Before each session, perform 3 rounds of Box Breathing (4 sec inhale, 4 hold, 4 exhale, 4 hold).
  • ACL Safety: If you feel sharp pain in your knee, stop the exercise immediately. Do not lift with your ego. Form is paramount.

Weekly Checkpoints (KPIs)

Log these every Sunday morning before eating or drinking.

  1. Bodyweight: Log in kg.
  2. Waist Measurement: Measure at the navel, log in cm.
  3. Performance KPI: Max reps of Bodyweight Pull-ups.
  4. Progress Photos: Front, back, and side. Same lighting and position each week.
